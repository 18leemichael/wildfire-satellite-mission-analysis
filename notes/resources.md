# Resources

## GOES, MODIS, and VIIRS

Website:
https://www.nesdis.noaa.gov/news/wildfires-space-how-the-view-above-helps-firefighters-the-ground

Purpose:
How satellites assist in wildfire monitoring in polar orbits and GEO

Useful Information:
- GOES remains in GEO and is used to see first embers or wildfires.
- GOES provides continuous, near-real time monitoring
- MODIS informed design of VIIRS
- VIIRS provides smaller, lower-temp fire detections day and night
