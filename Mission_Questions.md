# Mission Questions

## Project Goal

Determine whether a low Earth orbit satellite can effectively monitor wildfires.

## Questions to Answer

- What altitude should the satellite orbit at?
- How long is one orbit?
- How much of Earth's surface can the satellite see?
- How often would it pass over California?
- How many satellites are needed for near-continuous coverage?
- What tradeoffs exist between different orbit altitudes?

## Things I Don't Know Yet

- What altitude do current Earth observation satellites use?
- What sensor field of view is realistic?
- How is revisit time calculated?
